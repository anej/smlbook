About the authors

The authors of Spark are all principals in Lead Star, a global leadership development consulting firm with expertise in designing and delivering dynamic training programs.


Angie Morgan

Co-author of the business bestseller, Leading from the Front: No-Excuse Leadership Tactics for Women. In 2004, Angie co-founded Lead Star, a nationally recognized leadership development consulting firm that works with talented leaders within Fortune 500 companies, small and mid-sized businesses, leading nonprofits, government agencies, and prestigious academic institutions. Angie’s work has been recognized in the New York Times, Inc., Fast Company, Chicago Times, and on MSNBC, CNN and CNBC. Today she consults with senior executives on how to transform their cultures to ensure leadership is being expressed at every employment level.

Courtney Lynch

Co-author of Leading from the Front and co-founder of Lead Star. She routinely advises corporate, non-profit, government, and academic professionals on all aspects of leadership development. A successful entrepreneur, Courtney’s leadership transformed Lead Star from a start-up to a credible consulting firm. Her personal success has been recognized throughout her professional career, but most recently she was awarded the National Stevie Award for Best Female Entrepreneur and Businessweek profiled her achievements as a leadership expert.

Sean Lynch

Senior consultant at Lead Star and sought-after leadership expert. He is passionate about supporting others as they develop their leadership skills and achieve positive results for their organizations. After graduating from Yale University, Sean’s early leadership achievements came as an F-16 combat fighter pilot in the United States Air Force. A talented professional with nearly two decades of public and private sector leadership experience, Sean is a proven leader who advises senior leaders on an ongoing basis to help them transform their organizational culture.
